    About Me

    I'm Vishal Kaushik, a Digital Marketer dedicated to helping businesses grow smarter, not just louder. My journey began early through volunteering at NGOs and interning with Mahindra, experiences that deeply shaped my values around impact, clarity, and purpose. Today, I specialize in working with fitness studios, cafes, spiritual coaches, and local brands to create high-converting campaigns and build meaningful social media presences. I believe marketing is about more than just flashy ads — it’s about connecting with your audience through clear goals, bold creativity, and consistent effort that compounds over time.


    I combine data-driven strategies with authentic storytelling to deliver campaigns that truly resonate and convert. By deeply understanding your customers’ needs and behaviors, I craft messages that build trust and engagement, while continually optimizing performance to maximize your return on investment. Beyond short-term wins, I focus on fostering lasting relationships and brand loyalty, because sustainable growth comes from meaningful connections. Outside of work, I draw inspiration from exploring new fitness trends, discovering local cafes, and engaging in spiritual practices, all of which fuel my creativity and help me bring fresh, innovative ideas to every project.
